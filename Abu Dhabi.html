One of the most football geared Adidas stores in the UAE, always stocked with latest releases. If you're a messi fan you can find the boots he wears at this location, as he wears Adidas boots. If you want to get your hands on newer boots this is the store to go to, though the prices are steep.

This store has some of the cheapest prices on footbalL boots in Abu Dhabi. If you're looking for older relases that aren't sold in main outlets anymore, you can find them at this location. If you want to get your hands on newer boots this is not the store to go to, but if you want good prices its a great location.
