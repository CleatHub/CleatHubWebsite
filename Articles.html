Adidas Speedportal X

Overall, a very well rounded boot, but lacking in comfort and pace. Nothing groundbreaking, but still one of the best on sale this year.

An Uncomfortable Fit
Although this football boot uses a very comfortable artificial leather material, the shaping of it is a bit awkward. It felt quite tight in the wrong areas during my first 30 minutes of use, and while still usable , it was still very uncomfortable and not ideal for a match. The harddness of the carbon soleplate underneath the soft fabric also made the boot irritating to run with. However, after continuing to play for more than 30 minutes the boots started to "break in" and they became far more comfortable. I was able to enjoy the softness of the matieral though I still find the soleplate to be irritating. So, if you've just bought thiis boot and find it to be uncomfortable, don't let that put you off as that can change with time. However, I will say that this boot is thinner than most so consider getting a size above your normal one.
Shooting Like Salah
Now, after the boots started getting more comofortable, I got to trying them in practice, and WOW! Dribbling felt great with the hexagonal texture used on the outside of the ball that gave it more grip, clearly it was extremely efficient. However, dribbling still wasn't it's best aspect, shooting was! Its texture, hard soleplate, and stiff knitting greaetly improve dmy shot power, making ever finish seem more punchy and powerful. The material also seemed to let my feet almost wrap around the ball, greatly improving accuracy overall. It's clear why forwards like Mohammed Salah wear this football boot! Trivela's, volley's, power-shots, free-kicks, and even crossing and passing was all made easier with this boot. However after experimenting with the boot for 2 hours, I came to find that the soleplate was becoming more and more irritating, drastically lowering the comfort of the boot overall.
An All Round Solid Boot
Although I've only talked about good things so far, there are some negatives to the boot.First off, as mentioned earlier, the boot's soleplate makes the bottom very uncomfortable and paiinful to run in after weariing for multiple hours. Moreover,considering it is marketed as a speed boot, this boot is not that great for pace, as it's fabric isn't that light and it uses a carbon fiber soleplate which is heavy. Finally, the durabilty doesn't seem to be that great, with some of the outside texture already peeling off after 2 weeks of use. Overall, this boot iis still quite good. It seems to be perfect for midfielders who need to make accurate, powerful crosses but dont need to recieve them (which wouldd require speed). I could definitely see players like Kevin De Bruyne, Luka Modric, and Paul Pogba dominate their respective leagues using these boots, and can see children growing into top prospects with the help of these boots aswell. Here at CleatHub, we have given This football boot a 4.0 rating. Definitely had 4.5 star potential if it weren't for the comofort issues.
